ABOUT US

world travel nation

Unique online B2B global networking platform and online marketplace for tourism professionals.

Founded in 2017 and based in Dublin, Ireland, the WTN platform increases visibility with trade partners, manages existing and new relationships more efficiently, has a real-time access, easily manages a portfoli of products and services.

idea

Make it much easier, quicker, and more productive for tourism professionals from all over the world to connect, interact and collaborate with each other by bringing much of this interaction online in a useful and user-friendly way

solution

Connect all professionals of the global tourism industry online. Our platform is created for the travel industry and it’s needs, with simple but useful features that make the daily work of travel professionals easier, more productive and enjoyable

mission

Transform the way travel professionals around the world connect amongst themselves and share their expertise, experience, new products, market news and much more in a simple and efficient way

UNIQUE VALUE

Allowing travel professionals from all branches of the global tourism industry to find one another and to network in a unique global space that exists 24/7
